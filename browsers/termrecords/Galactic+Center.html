Editorial Note
Original term (Galaxy: center) rather ambiguous. When initial capitalized, "Galactic Center" refers to the central area of the Milky Way galaxy. I went with this meaning, as there is some overlap between generic galactic centers and galactic bulges, and because the Galactic Center is a significant topic in and of itself. May need clarification from IOP. -BG
